FASMA Festival

FASMA ("φάσμα"~fásma) is the greek word for spectrum, i.e. a condition that can be limited to a specific set of values but can also vary infinitely within a continuum. FASMA festival is a collective attempt to show the prism within which today's music manifests itself.
